About Us

In 2010, a simple family rest house, inspired by a "bahay-kubo" and made of anahaw, was built. Unfortunately, it was destroyed by a storm in 2014. It was renovated into a more modern style using stones, maintaining its unique theme but ensuring stability. An antique-style house was extended to include a function hall. Many are interested in celebrating special occasions in the newly built house and hall due to its captivating ambiance, appealing to a diverse audience.
